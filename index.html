Biography

Minseung Kim (Student Member, IEEE) received the B.S. degree in electrical and coltrol engineering and the M.S. degree in electrical engineering from the Mokpo National University, Muan, Republic of Korea, in 2022 and 2024, respectively. Since 2024 he has been working toward the Ph.D. degree in electrical and electronic engineering with the Hanyang University, Ansan, Republic of Korea. His current research interests include Grid-forming Inverter, Modular Multilevel Converter (MMC) and Machine Learning.
